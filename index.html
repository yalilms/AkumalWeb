Frequently
Asked Questions

01

How long does it take to manufacture my product?

Standard timelines range from 4 to 8 weeks from sample approval, depending on the complexity of the format and production volume. We will provide an exact schedule in your personalized quote.

02

What are the minimum order quantities (MOQ)?

MOQs vary by format and formula. Contact our team for a personalized, no-obligation quote tailored to your volume. We work with brands starting from small batches so you can test the market without large investments.

03

Can I use my own formula or do I need yours?

You can bring your own formula or let our team of nutritionists develop it from scratch based on your goals and target audience. In either case, the formula is yours.

04

What certifications do you have?

We manufacture under GMP (Good Manufacturing Practices) certification and comply with ISO quality management standards. Our processes ensure traceability, food safety, and quality control required by European regulations. Request our certificates by email.

05

Do you also handle packaging design?

Yes. We offer a comprehensive service that includes graphic design of labels complying with EU Regulation 1169/2011, packaging selection, and customization. We take care of everything so you only have to worry about selling.

06

Do you manufacture supplements for the international market?

Yes. We export to various European countries and have experience with labeling and registration regulations in the European Union, United Kingdom, and LATAM markets. As an OEM laboratory based in Europe, we support our clients in their international expansion.

07

What types of supplements can you manufacture as private label?

We manufacture a wide range: whey proteins (concentrate, isolate, hydrolysate), creatine, amino acids (BCAA, EAA), pre-workouts, collagen, fat burners, and sport vitamins. Available in powder, capsules, tablets, sticks, and bars.
