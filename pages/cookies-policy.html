Policy.

1. What are cookies?

A cookie is a file that is downloaded to your computer when accessing certain websites. Cookies allow a website, among other things, to store and retrieve information about the browsing habits of a user or their equipment.

2. Types of cookies used

  • Technical cookies: These are those that allow the user to navigate through a website, platform, or application and use the different options or services that exist therein.
  • Analysis cookies: These are those that, either processed by us or by third parties, allow us to quantify the number of users and thus carry out the measurement and statistical analysis of the use made by users of the service offered.

3. Cookies Management

You can allow, block, or delete cookies installed on your equipment by configuring the options of the browser installed on your computer. For more information, please contact us.
